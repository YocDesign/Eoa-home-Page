Not comfortable with computers

The platform is designed in such ways that even a person not very comfortable with internet, can use this platform with ease and without much help! Every function is obvious for its use and flow. The tool tips provided with the various functions helps the user as he/she navigates the application. The pictorial representation of the various function make the application crystal clear in use!

Too many sections and parts in the question paper

Normally the question paper which are used to tests contains various divisions (Sections) and subdivisions (parts) on the basis of subjects and question types (MCQ, True - false, matrix type etc.) More over the titles and their instructions are also there in the question paper. For typing and the offering hard copy to students is not a big deal but for internet based tests/exam it was really a cumbersome process till now. Most of the platform requires at least one day to one week time to manage a question paper before they offer online test to their students. Exam on air has solved it by creating the smartest way for uploading the question paper on web at one go. In fact the question paper which you can use for class room test can be directly be uploaded with no or hardly little modification and investing merely 5 minutes!

Different type of marking Schemes

There are different types of marking scheme associated with different type of questions. For example multiple options correct based questions and the marking is for exact match or partial marking on the basis of number of correct choices selected! The marking of one question on the basis of the response of the previous question whether it is correct or incorrect (Linked type questions). The marking on the choices of different options when none of the option is considered as incorrect (weighted marking). Some time few questions are erroneous in nature and the management has to take decisions for the evaluation of such questions. Exam on air provides facility for every scenario you may think of!

Different type of questions

There are different types of questions generally asked in the question papers (See the list). Exam on Air handles all type of questions maximum ease! You just keep on typing on word document and the application will automatically detect and produce before your students in the most appropriate way.

Habitual of conducting exam conventionally

Still the paper based exam is the most common in India. Not the faculty or management but also the students feel comfortable and also habitual of such system. The exam on air tries to simulate the same practices for internet based examination system. It allows the test director /administrators to use the same question paper (typed of conventional system) and the students to feel the OMR the filling while giving responses on the questions
